Lending Decisions: Credit score, income, employement history, debt-to-income ratio, etc.

Factors influencing credit score:
1. Payment history (on time or not)
2. Credit Utilization (Usage compared to limit) -> high means lower score
3. Length of history (How long account is open)
4. Types of credit (different types that borrower has like credit cards, loans, mortgages)
5. Recent inquiries (how many time borrower has applied for credit recently)

Factors influencing risk score: Payment history, credit utilization, length of credit history,
Types of credit, recent credit inquiries, income, debt-to-income ration, public records and other factors like
age(specially for young), education, geographic location.
